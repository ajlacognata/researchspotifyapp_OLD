AP Research Music-Politics Correspondence

Consent

Purpose of this study:

The purpose of this study is to collect data in order to compare one's political views with their preferred music taste in order to discover if there are any correlations between politics and their preffered music taste.

Anonymity:

All responses will remain anonymous and cannot be traced back to the participant. Responses can only be seen by the researched and will only be used within this research paper.

Participation Requirements:

To participate in this survey, you must be a high school student in the United States, have a Spotify Account, and agree to all conditions within the consent form.

Instructions

Participants will log into their Spotify account using the button below. This will give the researcher their top 50 tracks and artists of all time. The user will then be given a number to write down/remember. The user will also then be given a link to a Microsoft Forms survey. The first question will ask the participant to submit the number given to them. This survey will be used to assess the participant's political views, grade level, and collect any other non-required data such as sex and race/ethnicity.
